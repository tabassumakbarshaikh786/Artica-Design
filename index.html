Our Story

Designing Mumbai's Most
Beautiful Spaces

Founded in 2012, Artica Designs was born from a simple belief: that thoughtful design has the power to transform lives. What started as a small studio in Ghatkopar has grown into one of Mumbai's most trusted interior design firms.

Today, we are a team of over 40 passionate designers, architects, and project managers who bring together creativity, craftsmanship, and cutting-edge technology to create spaces that truly resonate.

From luxury residential interiors in South Mumbai to modern commercial fit-outs in BKC, our portfolio spans the breadth of what great design can achieve.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Artica Designs.

How long does a typical interior design project take? +

Project timelines vary depending on scope. A single room can take 4–6 weeks, a full apartment 3–5 months, and a large villa or commercial project 6–12 months. We always provide a detailed timeline upfront so you know exactly what to expect.

What is your pricing and how do you charge? +

Our pricing is transparent and project-based. We provide a detailed quote after the initial consultation. Fees typically cover design, 3D visualization, project management, and execution. We work across a range of budgets and always aim to deliver maximum value.

Do you provide a free consultation? +

Yes! We offer a free initial consultation — either at our Ghatkopar West studio or at your site. This helps us understand your requirements and give you an accurate sense of scope and cost before any commitment.

Can I see a 3D preview of my space before work begins? +

Absolutely. 3D visualization is a core part of our design process. We create photorealistic renders and walkthroughs of every space before a single wall is touched, so you can fully approve the design with confidence.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects? +

Yes, we have dedicated teams for both residential and commercial design. Our commercial portfolio includes offices, retail spaces, restaurants, showrooms, and hospitality venues across Mumbai and beyond.

What areas do you serve? +

Our primary service area is Greater Mumbai, including South Mumbai, Bandra, Andheri, Powai, Thane, Navi Mumbai, and of course Ghatkopar where our studio is located. We also take on select projects across Maharashtra.

What happens if I change my mind mid-project? +

Design is iterative and we understand that ideas evolve. Minor changes are accommodated smoothly. For significant scope changes, we'll discuss implications on timeline and cost transparently before proceeding.

Do you offer a warranty on your work? +

Yes, we stand behind our work. All civil work and installations come with a 1-year warranty. Modular furniture, electrical, and plumbing work come with manufacturer warranties in addition to our service guarantee.
